Refund Policy

Our 7-day money-back guarantee, eligibility rules, and how to request a refund.

Last updated: 21 May 2026

The short version: If you are a new customer and not satisfied with Inline IPTV, you can request a full refund within 7 days of your first paid subscription. No questions asked.

1. The 7-day money-back guarantee

Inline IPTV offers a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid subscriptions to give you confidence in choosing our service. If you are not satisfied with the Service within 7 days of your initial paid purchase, you may request a full refund.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund under our 7-day money-back guarantee, all of the following must be true:

  • The refund request is made within 7 days of your first paid subscription
  • You are a new customer (first-time subscriber). Renewals and repeat purchases are not eligible.
  • The request is submitted via email or WhatsApp with your order confirmation number
  • Your account has not been suspended or terminated for violation of our Terms of Service

3. Free trial vs. paid refund

The 24-hour free trial is separate from the refund policy — it does not require payment, so no refund is needed. The 7-day money-back guarantee applies only to paid subscriptions.

We strongly recommend testing the Service during the free trial before committing to a paid plan.

4. Situations where refunds are not available

Refunds are not available in the following situations:

  • Renewal payments — only the initial subscription is eligible
  • Repeat purchases — once you have been a customer, subsequent purchases are non-refundable
  • Requests made more than 7 days after the original purchase
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Issues caused by your internet connection, ISP throttling, or unsupported devices
  • Issues we have made reasonable efforts to resolve via our support team that you have refused
  • Promotional or discounted plans where the offer terms explicitly state "non-refundable"
  • Plans purchased through third-party resellers (refunds must be requested from the reseller)

5. How to request a refund

To request a refund, contact us via one of the following methods and include the information below:

  • Email: billing@inline-iptv.live
  • WhatsApp: +44 7848 933 246

Please include:

  • Your order confirmation number
  • The email address used for the subscription
  • A brief reason for the refund (this is optional but helps us improve the Service)

6. Processing time

  • Refund requests are reviewed within 2 business days
  • Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days
  • Processing time on your bank or card statement depends on your bank or card provider — please allow up to one full billing cycle

7. Partial refunds and service credits

In certain situations (for example, extended downtime exceeding our 99.9% uptime target), we may offer prorated refunds or service credits at our sole discretion. These are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

8. Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. We resolve almost all billing issues within 48 hours. Initiating a chargeback without first contacting support may result in immediate termination of your account and being barred from future subscriptions.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Changes apply only to subscriptions purchased after the change date — refund eligibility for existing subscriptions is governed by the policy in effect at the time of purchase.
